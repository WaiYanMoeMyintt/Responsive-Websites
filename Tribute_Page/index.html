Dr. Norman Borlaug

The man who saved a billion lives

Here's a time line of Dr. Borlaug's life:

  • 1914 - Born in Cresco, Iowa
  • 1970 - 1970 - receives the Nobel Peace Prize
  • 1976 - becomes a distinguished professor at Texas A&M University.
  • 2001 - Receives a Ph.D. in Genetics and Plant Pathology
  • 2002 - Discovers a way to grown wheat twice each season, doubling wheat yields
  • 2003 - receives the Nobel Peace Prize
  • 2006 - becomes a distinguished professor at Texas A&M University
  • 2008 - helps seven African countries dramatically increase their maize and sorghum yields
  • 2011 - Teen Choice Award - shared with Vin Diesel and Dwayne Johnson.
  • 2013 - becomes a distinguished professor at Texas A&M University.
  • 2015 - dies at the age of 95.
